Why it exists

A place to think out loud.

Most "tech blogs" are written by big companies for SEO traffic. They're polished, generic and forgettable.

techdesk is the opposite. It's where you write the article you wish you'd found on Google at 2am while debugging. It's the raw, real, sometimes-messy story of how something actually got built.

Whether you're a student writing your first post, or a senior engineer sharing a war story - this is your desk too.

What we stand for

Three rules of the desk.

Open knowledge

Every post is free to read. No paywalls, no signup-walls to read articles. Knowledge spreads better when it's free.

Real over polished

A messy how-I-fixed-it post beats a sanitized tutorial every time. Write like you'd explain it to a friend.

Help juniors

Remember when you were stuck on your first bug? Write the post you needed. Someone, somewhere, is stuck on the same thing right now.

What to write about

Anything tech. Seriously.

If it has anything to do with code, computers or building things - it belongs here.
